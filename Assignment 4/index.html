The Haskell Plaza Story

Welcome to the Haskell Plaza! A point of campus that we all have traversed through and might have even made a stop on a warm, sunny day to study or soak in the sun. But our connection to our phones has us so sucked into the screen that we forget to look up and look around.
Today we will explore the plaza and the mini bronze people created by Tom Otterness, we will pair up to collaborate on writing our own stories for the bronze figures.

Rules

  • Each figure or set of figures must have a name
  • Each figure or set of figures must have a personality
  • Keep each story point within 200 words since we are limited on time
  • After you're done you will come back to view your story come together on the Home Page
  • Keep in mind that your story must flow based on the order you started in within this plaza
