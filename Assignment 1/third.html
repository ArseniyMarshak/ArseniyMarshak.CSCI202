As the third year rolled around, I was finally in my major for Management Information Systems and I began working on developing my skills as a professional. Continuing through as the Vice President of AKPsi, I had now set my eyes on becoming the President. Through a lot of work, I was nominated and elected the President in my third year. Furthermore, the friendships continued to grow and I had the opportunity to experience my first set of MIS courses. I learned about Systems Design, Programming, and Database development. Along with my classes, I competed in two case competition, one in Fall and one in Winter. Both were very stressful and fun, and I am very grateful to have competed in such business competitions. Through out the year I was also out on my internship search, hoping to get another opportunity to gain real world experience and build up my skills. With the help of one of my friends, who recommended me for her internship role, I secured a position at a software company that develops software for non-profits, grant makers, and community foundations. It is an opportunity, I am very grateful for as it has allowed me to learn and grow more as a professional.
