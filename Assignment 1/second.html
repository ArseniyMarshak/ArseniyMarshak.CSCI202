In my second year I continued as a member of AKPsi. Moved in with one my close friends into my first apartment, and settled in for another year of learning. I had also discovered the sport of power lifting, and began training for my first meet which was being held at Western. After a long training period I earned 3rd place and had a chance to compete in front of a great number of people. Classes got harder but I met more friends. I got stuck with a couple tough classes but I powered through and thankfully, with the help of having my friends in those classes, it was not too bad. I was also nominated and elected the Vice President of AKPsi and continued on as I searched for my first internship. With my friends, I also had a chance to learn the game of softball (and baseball) by playing intramural softball in Spring quarter. It was very fun learning a new sport and also understanding a new sport that at first glance seems so confusing if you've never played After securing an internship through close connections, I had the opportunity to travel to Warsaw, Poland where I worked at a real estate firm. It was a very rewarding experience where I learned a lot about business and real estate. Outside of work I also had the opportunity to explore the city and get involved in the culture which is so close to mine.
